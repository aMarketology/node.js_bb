### 2. BlackBook Tokens ($BB) ‚Äî Sweepstakes Entries
**Purpose:** FREE sweepstakes entries that can win real USDC prizes.

**Key Characteristics:**
- ‚úÖ **100% FREE** ‚Äî never purchased directly
- ‚úÖ Can be redeemed for USDC winnings
- ‚úÖ Distributed through promotions, bonuses, and daily rewards
- ‚ùå **Never sold or purchased**

**Legal Classification:**
- Sweepstakes entries (like McDonald's Monopoly pieces)
- "No Purchase Necessary" model
- Free Alternative Method of Entry (AMOE) compliant

**How Users Acquire $BB:**
1. **Sign-Up Bonus:** 100 $BB upon registration
2. **Daily Login Rewards:** 10 $BB per day
3. **Fan Coin Purchase Bonus:** Buy FC, receive FREE $BB
   - Example: Buy 10,000 FC ($10) ‚Üí Get 50 FREE $BB
4. **Promotional Giveaways:** Social media contests, referrals
5. **Mail-In Request:** Physical mail option (AMOE compliance)

**Why This Is Legal:**
- Users can obtain $BB **without spending money**
- Eliminates "consideration" element of gambling (Prize + Chance + Consideration = Gambling)
- Follows sweepstakes precedent: Publishers Clearing House, McDonald's Monopoly

**Use Cases:**
- Enter skill-based fantasy contests
- Win real USDC prizes
- Redeem for cash via blockchain settlement

### 3. USDC ‚Äî Redemption Currency
**Purpose:** Stablecoin used for settling real-money winnings.

**Key Characteristics:**
- ‚úÖ 1:1 peg to US Dollar
- ‚úÖ Blockchain-based (transparent settlement)
- ‚úÖ Withdrawable to user's wallet or bank account
- ‚ùå Cannot be purchased within PRISM (use external exchanges)

**Legal Classification:**
- Digital dollar (stablecoin)
- Settlement currency for prizes
- Regulated under existing crypto frameworks

**How Users Receive USDC:**
1. Win fantasy contests using $BB entries
2. USDC winnings sent to user's BlackBook Wallet
3. Withdraw to personal wallet or exchange for USD

**Settlement Flow:**
```
User Wins Contest ‚Üí $BB Redeemed ‚Üí USDC Sent to Wallet ‚Üí User Withdraws
```

## üõ°Ô∏è Legal Framework: The "Double Shield"

### Shield #1: Sweepstakes Model
**BlackBook Tokens ($BB) are FREE**
- Eliminates "consideration" (payment) element
- Follows sweepstakes legal precedent
- "No Purchase Necessary" compliance

**Precedent:**
- McDonald's Monopoly (win without purchase)
- Publishers Clearing House (mail-in entries)
- Radio station giveaways (call-in entries)

### Shield #2: Skill-Based Fantasy Games
**Contests require skill, not chance**
- Fantasy rosters demand player knowledge
- Tactical Bingo requires strategic decisions
- Head-to-Head Duels reward expertise

**Legal Exemptions:**
- Texas Penal Code ¬ß47.02(a)(4) (fantasy sports carve-out)
- UIGEA (2006) federal exemption
- Skill predominance over chance

### Combined Defense
```
Sweepstakes (no purchase) + Fantasy (skill-based) = NOT GAMBLING
```

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### "If I buy Fan Coins, am I buying BlackBook tokens?"
**No.** You're purchasing FC (entertainment currency). The $BB is a **FREE BONUS**, like a sweepstakes prize. This separation keeps the platform 100% legal.

### "Can I convert Fan Coins to BlackBook tokens?"
**No.** FC and $BB are completely separate currencies. You cannot exchange one for the other. $BB is only distributed for free.

### "How do you make money if $BB is free?"
**Revenue from Fan Coin sales.** We sell FC for entertainment purposes. The $BB bonuses are promotional giveaways (like free samples).

### "Can I buy USDC within PRISM?"
**No.** USDC is only earned as contest winnings. To acquire USDC outside the platform, use Coinbase or other exchanges.

### "What happens to my Fan Coins if I win a contest?"
**Nothing.** Fan Coins are for entertainment only. Contest winnings are paid in USDC (if you entered with $BB).

### "Can I withdraw Fan Coins?"
**No.** FC has no cash value and cannot be withdrawn. It's purely for entertainment (like arcade tokens).

### "How do I get more $BB without spending money?"
1. Daily login (10 $BB/day)
2. Social media promotions
3. Referral bonuses
4. Mail-in request (AMOE compliance)

### "Is this legal in Texas?"
**Yes.** Our dual-shield approach (sweepstakes + fantasy) complies with:
- Texas Penal Code ¬ß47.02 (fantasy exemption)
- FTC sweepstakes regulations
- UIGEA (2006) federal law
